Do you ship internationally?

  • Currently, we only offer shipping within India. We understand that our international customers are eager to receive our products, and we are actively working on expanding our shipping options to reach more countries in the near future.

  • For now, if you are located outside of India, we recommend using a package forwarding service that can provide a local address for shipping within India. Once your order is delivered to that address, the forwarding service can send it to your international location.

  • We appreciate your understanding and support as we strive to grow our services. If you have any further questions or suggestions, please feel free to reach out to our customer support team!

Can I change my order?

Yes, you can change your order! However, changes can only be made within a limited time frame after your purchase. If you realize you need to modify your order, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible.

What payment methods do you accept?

We strive to make your shopping experience as convenient as possible by offering a variety of payment methods. Currently, we support:

  • Credit and Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.
  • Digital Wallets: Pay Google Pay, and Apple Pay.
  • Bank Transfers: Direct bank transfers for secure payments.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Available in select areas for your convenience.
